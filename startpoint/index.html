Info:

Audio w/ Binaurals: The audio player on this website features tracks overlaid with binaural tones. Binaural tones have are simple tones that when audible in both ears can promote the brains ability enter different states of consciousness, there is evidence that supports listening certain binaurals can promote focus, memory retention, stress relief and many other benefits. While the effects of these binaurals may seem negligable to many people, others swear by their effectiveness. I designed this site so that individuals would have a site they could go to when they wanted to remind themselves to focus on a task.

Lights Out: Use the 'lights out' button when working late at night or if your eyes hurting from excess blue light from your computer monitor.

Pomodoro

The Pomodoro timer is a productivity timer that is designed to help you be at your most productive. A typical pomodoro is 30 minutes long, 25 minutes of work, then 5 minutes of break forcing you to move around and stay active. Some people see pomodoro timers as a life hack and believe that it helps them focus better and for a longer period of time.
